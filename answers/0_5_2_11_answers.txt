- Use LinkedIn’s job search and apply relevant filters (keywords, location, experience level, industry, company, remote/on-site, date posted) to narrow postings to your skills and qualifications.
- Save searches and turn on job alerts to get notified of new matches.
- Follow target companies and join industry groups to see openings and updates.
- Optimize your profile (headline, summary, skills, endorsements, up-to-date experience) so recruiters and job recommendations match your qualifications.
- Use “Easy Apply” where available and tailor your resume and cover message for each posting.
- Leverage your network: ask connections for referrals or insights into posted roles.
- Review LinkedIn’s recommended jobs and the “skills match” indicators; use LinkedIn Learning to build any missing skills.
