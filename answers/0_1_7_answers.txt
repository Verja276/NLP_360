- Use the APR to view and understand your degree and program requirements.  
- Map your completed, in‑progress, and planned courses to those requirements to see which are satisfied and which remain.  
- Build and adjust a term‑by‑term course plan interactively to explore different course choices and sequences.  
- Use the APR as a starting point for making informed decisions about your program.  
- Consult an Academic Advisor for formal course planning, degree planning, and graduation checks — the APR does not replace individual academic advising.
