9.
Finances

10.
- Itemize all term costs: tuition and mandatory fees, student fees (U‑Pass), residence or housing, food, textbooks and supplies, transportation, insurance/health, personal/technology expenses, and program‑specific fees.  
- Use SFU’s cost‑estimator and financial readiness pages (learn more) and consult Enrolment Services for exact tuition amounts and payment plans.  
- Apply for financial aid early: scholarships, bursaries, grants, and student loans — check deadlines and requirements.  
- Seek income opportunities through on‑campus work, co‑op placements, and part‑time jobs; contact Career services for help.  
- Build a term budget: total expected costs minus expected income/aid; break into monthly amounts, prioritize essentials, and set aside an emergency fund.  
- Review and adjust your budget each term and use SFU supports (Financial Aid/Enrolment Services/Career services) for guidance.
