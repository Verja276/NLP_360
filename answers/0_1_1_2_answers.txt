1.
goSFU is SFU’s 24/7 information management system for course registration, viewing grades, tracking degree progress, ordering transcripts, and applying for graduation.  
It is available to SFU students, faculty, and staff (with access also provided to applicants, alumni, and certain Fraser International College students for specific services).
