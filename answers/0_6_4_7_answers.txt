21.
- Eligible international students are automatically enrolled by SFU and will receive an enrolment email from Guard.me.  
- Activate your Guard.me account via the Guard.me/SFU portal using your SFU student number (and the one-time registration details in the enrolment email); create a password.  
- Download your Guard.me digital ID card and coverage certificate from your Guard.me account.  
- To opt out, submit proof of comparable coverage to SFU by the opt-out deadline.  
- For help: intl_advising@sfu.ca or 778‑782‑4232.
