The Multifaith Centre is an SFU centre offering faith care and wellbeing (pastoral) support to students, staff, and faculty, providing inclusive spiritual, emotional and social support across religious and non‑religious traditions.

Services: one‑on‑one Chaplain Chat and support, spiritual direction and pastoral care (a listening ear), meetings with a diverse team of chaplains, appointment booking and drop‑in/contactable chaplaincy support.
