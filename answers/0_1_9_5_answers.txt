1.
- You should have received your parchment at your Convocation ceremony or had it mailed afterwards.

- To replace or request additional copies: complete the Parchment Replacement Form, include your student number and your signature, and pay the required fees. All outstanding accounts must be cleared before release.

- If you don’t know your student number, request it via the SFU alumni student-number request form.

- Do not request parchments by phone. For email requests: make payment via internet banking before emailing the signed form to urecords@sfu.ca (do NOT email credit card details).

- In person: order or pick up at Registrar & Information Services counters (Burnaby, Surrey, Vancouver) with photo ID. Cash is not accepted at counters; pay by cheque, debit (in person), Visa or MasterCard.

- Processing: requests are processed monthly on the 15th (payments must be received before the 15th). Rush processing (additional $50 + $3.50 PST) is available only in person or by fax and guarantees processing within five business days; rush does not include parchment cost or priority delivery.

- For rush verification or general inquiries contact Undergraduate Records at 778.782.6930 or phone 778.782.4969.

- Pickup: you will be emailed when your parchment is ready; photo ID required. You can authorize a third party via a Privacy Service Indicator in Student Center or a signed letter of authorization (the third party must show photo ID).

- Mailing: priority/courier (Canada Post Xpresspost) is available for an additional charge and provides tracking.

2.
1. Complete the Parchment Replacement Form.  
2. Include your student number and sign the form (if you don’t know your student number, request it via SFU alumni student-number request).  
3. Ensure all outstanding accounts are cleared.  
4. Pay in full (cheque, Visa or MasterCard; debit accepted in person; or internet banking prior to emailing the form). Do NOT email credit card information.  
5. Submit the form by email to urecords@sfu.ca, by mail, fax, or in person at the Registrar & Information Services counters (Burnaby, Surrey, Vancouver).  
6. If you need rush processing, request in person or by fax (rush $50 + $3.50 PST; not available for email/mail) and contact Undergraduate Records to verify (778‑782‑6930).  
7. Provide photo identification to pick up the parchment; to authorize a third party, add a Privacy Service Indicator or provide a signed letter of authorization and the third party’s photo ID.
