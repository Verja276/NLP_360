Variable costs: expenses that fluctuate based on use or consumption and are less predictable or adjustable (e.g., food, entertainment, transportation, clothing, one‑off supplies).

- Learn to budget and build healthy financial habits.  
- Identify and separate fixed costs (predictable, term/monthly) and variable costs (fluctuating), and consider examples of each.
