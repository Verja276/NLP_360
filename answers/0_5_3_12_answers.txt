- Notify your instructors and exam coordinators well in advance and request accommodation for the holy day.
- Check exam and assignment schedules early and ask to reschedule or arrange alternative assessments if needed.
- Follow your university’s formal accommodation procedures and provide any required documentation.
- Plan coursework and study time ahead of the holy day to avoid conflicts with deadlines.
- Arrange with classmates for notes or recordings of missed classes.
- Use Multifaith Centre resources and read the tips they provide for students observing holy days.
- Contact chaplains for spiritual support or to discuss options (multi_faith@sfu.ca, 778.782.3180).
- Find and use multifaith rooms/prayer spaces on campus as needed.
