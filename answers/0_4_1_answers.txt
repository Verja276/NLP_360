10.
1) Process for applying to co‑op:
- Review the co‑op roadmap and program-specific requirements (fees, tuition, deadlines).  
- Attend co‑op info sessions and explore program resources.  
- Start your application and contact your co‑op program office or meet with a co‑op advisor (in‑person or virtually) for one‑on‑one support.  
- Use co‑op supports to prepare your resume, cover letter and interview skills.

2) Next steps after applying:
- Follow the program’s timeline and guidance on what to expect while participating.  
- Continue meeting with co‑op staff/advisors for planning and support.  
- Prepare for work‑term placement(s) (interviews, employer matching, onboarding).  
- Complete work terms to gain experience, references and potential full‑time offers; reflect on outcomes to inform your academic/career plans.
