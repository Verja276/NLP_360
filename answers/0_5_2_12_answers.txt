The FMNCP is a healthy-eating initiative that supports farmers’ markets and strengthens food security in British Columbia by providing coupons that can be spent at participating BCAFM member farmers’ markets on items like vegetables, fruits, nuts, eggs, dairy, cut herbs, meat and fish. Eligible recipients are lower-income families, pregnant people, and seniors participating in community partner food‑literacy programs.
