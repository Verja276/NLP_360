20.
A mandatory program that provides SFU students with unlimited access to public transit in Metro Vancouver using a Compass Card.

21.
Go to SFU's U‑Pass BC webpage to learn about the program and check your eligibility, fees, and FAQs.

22.
- Make sure your U‑Pass BC is loaded onto an active Compass Card (issued/activated through SFU).
- Tap your Compass Card on the card reader when boarding buses or at SkyTrain/SeaBus fare gates/validators; hold until you see a green light/beep.
- You don’t need to tap off for U‑Pass trips covered by the program.
- Carry your Compass Card while travelling and present it to transit staff or inspectors if asked.
- If your card is lost, stolen, or not working, contact SFU/TransLink to replace the card and transfer your U‑Pass.
