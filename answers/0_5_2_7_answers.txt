2.
Former youth in care in BC (of any age as of Aug 1, 2023) studying at the undergraduate level, full-time or part-time.

The Provincial Tuition Waiver Program (PTWP) waives tuition and fees for eligible former youth in care.

Contact fiassist@sfu.ca (ATTN YIC Supports Navigator) for next steps and supports.

Financial Aid & Awards will contact PTWP recipients annually about the Learning for Future Grant (LFG).

The Learning for Future Grant (LFG) provides $3,500 per program year to students meeting PTWP eligibility.

No application is required for the LFG; students are automatically considered (normally in week 3 of the Spring term, subject to funding).

SAJE provides Life‑Skills, Training and Cultural Connections (LSTCC) funding and a Mental Health and Counselling Benefit for youth/young adults with care experience.

The Agreements with Young Adults (AYA) program can help cover costs such as housing, child care, tuition and health care while returning to school or attending approved programs.
