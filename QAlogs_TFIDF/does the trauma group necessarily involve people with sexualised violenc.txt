Question: What is the purpose of the trauma-informed group for students impacted by sexualized violence?
Answer: The purpose of the trauma-informed group is to provide a gender-inclusive safe space for students impacted by sexualized violence to discuss impacts, practice coping strategies, learn about trauma and recovery, coping skills, and emotional regulation, and receive support on their healing journey from a registered clinical counsellor.
