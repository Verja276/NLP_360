CAL BASICS

To receive accommodation support from CAL, you need to be registered with us.  Registration is a process that takes some time; understanding what we do and the steps to become registered can help you prepare for a smooth journey to support!

What We Do

Review documentation to verify and confirm presence of a formally diagnosed disability.

What We Don't Do

If we aren’t the right resource for you at this time, we will always point you towards the best campus support to address your concerns.
