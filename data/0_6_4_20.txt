SFU International (SFUi), under the VP Research, develops and implements strategic plans and policies that enhance the university’s well-established reputation as a leader in the field of international post-secondary education and engagement and provides university-wide strategic leadership in the development, integration, and promotion of international partnerships, initiatives, projects, and programs.

The International Services for Students (ISS) office is pleased to work with SFU faculty wishing to develop international and intercultural learning opportunities for students. 

As part of our work supporting students, International Services for Students (ISS) also offers resources and support for staff and faculty who work with international and newcomer students.
