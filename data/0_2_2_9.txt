SFU offers a variety of financial assistance opportunities to Canadian Indigenous students. Indigenous can be contextualized as "Indigenous peoples of Canada" to refer specifically to First Nations, Métis, and Inuit peoples.

Indigenous scholarships and awards opportunities for current students.

Entrance award for Indigenous high school applicants. 

Canadian Indigenous students may be eligible for third party sponsorship from a First Nations, Metis or Inuit organization.

Bursaries and work-study opportunities for undergraduate and graduate students.

Government assistance, including loans and grants.

Funding is available for graduate students attending and participating in a conference. For eligibility information and how to apply, please see the Office of Graduate and Post-Doctoral Studies.

Emergency funding may be available to students with a sudden exceptional circumstances. Please contact the Indigenous Student Centre (ISC) for further information.

Financial Aid works in collaboration with our Indigenous Student Centre (ISC)
