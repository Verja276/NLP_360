Thank you for your interest in attending the RTW Information Sessions.

The RTW Information Session is designed to help you understand your academic standing and the available options for students with a Required to Withdraw (RTW) academic standing, including college readmission and the Back on Track (BOT) program. Please note, attending the information session is a mandatory first step for students interested in joining BOT. 

The eligibility and program requirements for the BOT program will also be reviewed in depth during the information session. 

Eligibility and program requirements for the BOT Program are reviewed in depth during the session, and attending this session is a mandatory first step for students interested in joining BOT. 

Spring 2026 Required to Withdraw information sessions:

UPCOMING IN-PERSON SESSIONS

UPCOMING ONLINE SESSIONS

You must register for the session through Advisor Link (https://community.sfu.ca/advising/s/info-sessions). Once logged in, scroll down on the home page and click on "Required to Withdraw Information Sessions."

If you’re having trouble accessing Advisor Link or have any accessibility needs, please email success@sfu.ca with your preferred session date and time. We will be happy to assist you with registration.

If you are interested in deferring your start in the BOT program to Summer 2026, please review the information here and email success@sfu.ca to inquire about your options and eligibility.

We look forward to connecting with you soon.
