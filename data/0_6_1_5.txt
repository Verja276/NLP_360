Simon Fraser University acknowledges and affirms the rights of students with disabilities to have equal access to the university and to be welcomed as fully participating members of the university community. The Centre for Accessible Learning (CAL) provides support and advocacy for students with disabilities. Our primary responsibility is determining reasonable academic accommodations for registered students with documented disabilities. 

The information provided in these pages is intended to serve as a resource for university faculty and staff, and to help build a strong community where students with disabilities are fully integrated and have equal educational access. We hope this information enhances understanding of individual rights and responsibilities in relation to disability services on campus.

Please see the student accommodations or faculty and instructor FAQs page for more information.
