Discover a global education: develop skills, expand perspective, create connections.

Exchanges are a way to live abroad and immerse yourself in the host culture, while internationalizing your degree and resume. Gain credit towards your SFU degree by studying abroad at one of SFU's 130+ exchange partner institutions, while in most cases paying your regular tuition to SFU you can take courses taught in English at most institutions. Virtual Exchanges are offered at select partner institutions.

Exchange offers opportunities to:

Build your network abroad

Connect with professors, future employers, and fellow students from around the world

Explore global opportunities

Connect with future employers, or test out studying at a host institution you are considering for graduate school

Gain international perspective on your degree

Study your subject matter through a new lens, or study new topics.

Experience a new culture


Live and study in a new culture,  learn or practice a language.


Develop global competencies valued by employers

Develop your adaptability, creative problem-solving and  intercultural communication skills as you navigate life and school abroad.
