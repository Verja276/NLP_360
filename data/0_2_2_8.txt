SFU recognizes the demands that International students undertake during the course of their educational journey. At SFU, the Financial Aid and Awards Office is committed to providing funding opportunities for International students to financially assist qualified applicants in reaching their educational goals.

See all funding options for all International, including US, students. 

Private education loans can be used by families to help pay the cost to attend college when other financial aid is not enough.                                                                                                                                            

Federal Direct Loans are available to students through the U.S. Department of Education in the form of both subsidized and/or unsubsidized loans.     

Resources for undergraduate and graduate US students.
